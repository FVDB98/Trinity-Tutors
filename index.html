In broad terms, our home is in the village of Stockton on the Forest, about 6km from York city centre. It takes less than 15 minutes to be in the heart of the city. We have 6 bedrooms available for students, including singles, a twin, a double and a king sized room. All students have desks in their rooms and plenty of hanging/storage space. Our home is detached with a beautiful garden.

In terms of meals, we offer a continental breakfast during the week and a cooked "brunch" at weekends. Weekday lunches are one or two courses, and always freshly prepared, healthy and nutritious.

Weekday dinners are three courses. Weekend dinners are three courses and, of course, there is always a traditional Sunday roast dinner! All dietary requirements are catered for. There is always a supply of fresh fruit and other snacks for students to help themselves to, including fruit juice and other soft drinks.
